About This Tool

The Single-Trade Crypto Profit Calculator was created to solve a widespread issue in cryptocurrency trading: most traders dramatically overestimate their actual returns. While exchange charts display impressive gross percentage gains, real-world deductions from trading fees and capital gains taxes often reduce profits by 10–30% or more. This tool delivers complete transparency by calculating your true net profit after all costs.

Designed as a fully client-side, static web application built with SvelteKit and Bootstrap, it requires no registration, no data storage, and no server interaction beyond loading the page itself. Every computation — including gross profit, tax deductions, net profit, ROI, gross profit percentage, and break-even sell price — occurs instantly in your browser. This privacy-first architecture ensures that your trade details never leave your device.

The calculator supports accurate modeling of real-world scenarios with separate buy and sell fee percentages (reflecting maker/taker differences), customizable tax rates, and detailed breakdowns. It is ideal for analyzing executed trades, planning hypothetical positions, or understanding the true cost of frequent trading. Features like break-even price calculation help set realistic targets and informed stop-loss levels.

This project draws direct inspiration from common trading pitfalls explored in our blog series, such as ignoring fee compounding, misunderstanding short-term vs long-term tax implications, and failing to account for round-trip costs. By making precise calculation accessible and instantaneous, the tool helps traders develop disciplined habits and more realistic expectations.

Whether you are a beginner discovering the impact of fees and taxes for the first time or an experienced trader refining position sizing and target setting, accurate net profit awareness is foundational to long-term success. This calculator turns educational insights into practical daily utility.

We are committed to keeping this tool free, open-source, and ad-free forever. The full source code is available on GitHub for review, contribution, or self-hosting. Thank you for using it — we hope it helps you make smarter, more profitable trading decisions.

How To Use

1
Enter Trade Details

Input the token symbol (optional), quantity traded, buy price per unit, and sell price per unit. Use actual executed values for post-trade analysis or hypothetical numbers for planning future trades.

2
Set Fees & Tax Rate

Adjust buy and sell fee percentages to match your exchange (defaults to 0.1%). Many platforms charge different rates for maker vs taker orders. Enter your applicable capital gains tax rate to see the true net outcome.

3
View Detailed Results

Click "Calculate Profit" to instantly reveal total cost (with buy fee), proceeds (after sell fee), gross profit, tax deduction, net profit, ROI, profit percentage, and break-even sell price. Positive values appear in green, losses in red.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

Yes — completely. This tool runs 100% in your browser. No information is sent to any server, stored, or tracked. Inputs are processed locally and cleared on page refresh.

Why are buy and sell fees separate?

Many exchanges charge different rates depending on whether your order adds (maker) or removes (taker) liquidity. Separate fields provide maximum real-world accuracy.

How accurate is the tax calculation?

It uses a simplified flat-rate model based on your input. Real tax situations vary by jurisdiction, holding period, and individual circumstances. Always consult a tax professional.

What does break-even sell price mean?

It is the exact price you must sell at to recover your total buy cost (including buy fee) after deducting the sell fee — your true zero-profit point.

Can I use this for planning future trades?

Absolutely. Enter hypothetical prices and fees to model potential outcomes, test targets, and understand risk-reward before committing capital.

Is this tool free?

Yes — forever. No ads, no limits, no registration. It is fully open-source on GitHub.
