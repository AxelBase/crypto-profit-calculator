Formulas & Breakdown

Deep dive into the mathematics driving your profit calculations.

Core Calculation sequence

Buy Value
Base Buy + Buy Fee Amount

The absolute cost to enter the position.

Sell Value
Base Sell - Sell Fee Amount

The actual cash returned after exit fees.

Gross Profit
Sell Value - Buy Value

Profit before the tax man takes his share.

Net Profit
Gross Profit - Tax Deduction

The final amount available for reinvestment.

Detailed Examples

Example: Winning Trade
  • Buy: $5,000 (0.1% fee) → Cost: $5,005
  • Sell: $6,000 (0.1% fee) → Net: $5,994
  • Net Profit: $791.20 (after 20% tax)
Example: Losing Trade
  • Buy: $3,000 (0.2% fee) → Cost: $3,006
  • Sell: $2,500 (0.2% fee) → Net: $2,495
  • Net Loss: -$511.00 (No tax applied)
