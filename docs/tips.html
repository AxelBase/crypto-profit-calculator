Pro Trading Tips

Master your strategy and protect your capital with these industry best practices.

Fee Efficiency

Use limit orders to reduce fees and choose exchanges that offer fee rebates for high-volume traders.

Risk Management

Never risk more than 1-2% of your total portfolio on a single trade. Preservation is key.

Long-term Gains

Holding assets for over a year can significantly reduce your tax burden in many jurisdictions.

Secure Storage

Keep your long-term holds in cold storage. Only keep what you are actively trading on exchanges.
