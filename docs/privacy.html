Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 24, 2025

Welcome to the Single-Trade Crypto Profit Calculator, a free, open-source, client-side web tool designed to help cryptocurrency traders accurately calculate profits after fees and taxes. We take your privacy seriously and have built this tool with a zero-data-collection philosophy. This Privacy Policy explains exactly how we handle (or rather, do not handle) your information.

No Personal Data Collected

This tool operates entirely in your browser. When you enter buy/sell prices, quantities, fees, or tax rates, all calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the data you input. There are no servers receiving your trade details, no databases storing your calculations, and no logs of your activity.

We do not gather:

  • Personal information such as names, email addresses, or wallet addresses.
  • Usage data including IP addresses, browser type, device information, or location.
  • Trade data — all inputs remain strictly on your device and are cleared when you close or refresh the page.
  • Analytics or tracking data — we do not use Google Analytics, cookies, pixels, or any third-party tracking services.

How the Tool Works

Every feature — from basic profit calculation to scenario planning and break-even analysis — runs using JavaScript in your browser. No data ever leaves your device. Even features like resetting the form or navigating pages happen client-side without any server communication beyond loading the initial static files.

No Cookies or Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, localStorage for persistent data, session storage for tracking, or any other tracking mechanisms. Your experience is completely stateless and anonymous.

Third-Party Services

The site uses only Bootstrap via CDN for styling and no other external services that could collect data. Open-source libraries are loaded statically and do not track users.

Security and Your Responsibility

Since no data is transmitted, there is no risk of interception. However, we recommend using the tool on a secure device and network, especially when handling real trade information.

Children's Privacy

This tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Your privacy is our priority — we collect nothing, store nothing, and track nothing.
